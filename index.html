DAWGSPLORATION

we call it! Exploring an environment the 'dog' way! When our beloved dogs share our urban environment with us, they get food, shelter and loads of love, What they miss, however, is the natural 'animalistic' use of their senses in a 'free environment' and the choice to actually explore on their own in a safe, organic space! Scentral Park is a labor of love, made to fill this very need of our four-legged furry friends. A safe place where they move around freely and choose the activities they may like to engage in, with no (or minimal) help from their humans 🙂
